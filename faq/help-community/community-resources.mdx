# Community Guides and Documentation

This page highlights high-value third-party documentation, contributed guides, and channels for learning from experienced GoogleTest and GoogleMock users. It is designed to empower both new and seasoned developers to deepen their expertise and stay informed about best practices, advanced techniques, and community-driven solutions.

---

## Why Use Community Documentation?

While official GoogleTest documentation forms the core of your learning, community guides can provide invaluable insights through:

- Practical, real-world examples
- Advanced tips and tricks
- Troubleshooting experience beyond the official scope
- Updates on evolving usage patterns and integrations

Leveraging these resources accelerates your mastery and integrates you into the broader GoogleTest ecosystem.

---

## Featured Community Resources

### 1. Googlemock Insights by ElectricRCAircraftGuy
[Googlemock Insights](https://github.com/ElectricRCAircraftGuy/eRCaGuy_dotfiles/blob/master/googletest/insights.md) is a detailed, community-curated guide focusing on the mocking aspects of GoogleTest, authored by an experienced user. It covers advanced mocking patterns, setup of expectations, common pitfalls, and performance considerations.

Benefits:
- Learn nuanced usage of `EXPECT_CALL` and `ON_CALL`
- Understand mock strictness modes like NiceMock, NaggyMock, and StrictMock
- Explore troubleshooting advice from real-world debugging
