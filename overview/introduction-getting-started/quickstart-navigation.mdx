# Quickstart & Next Steps

Welcome to the GoogleTest Quickstart & Next Steps guide—a compass to help you efficiently navigate your journey from first setup to expert mastery. Whether you're a newcomer eager to dive in or a seasoned user looking to deepen your skills, this page directs you precisely where to go next for practical learning and productive adoption.

## Getting Started: Your Initial Steps

Begin your adventure with essential primers designed to make your first test experience smooth and successful:

- **[GoogleTest Primer](primer.md)**: Learn the basics of writing and running your first tests to build a solid foundation.
- **[GoogleMock for Dummies](gmock_for_dummies.md)**: Discover how to create and use mock objects with intuitive explanations and examples.

These guides focus on user workflows and hands-on examples, ensuring you quickly get real value from GoogleTest and GoogleMock.

## Deepening Your Expertise: Detailed User Guides

When you're ready to expand your knowledge beyond the basics, explore targeted guides that explore GoogleTest and GoogleMock features in depth:

- **[Mocking Cookbook](gmock_cook_book.md)**: Gain practical recipes and best practices for advanced mocking strategies and troubleshooting.
- **[Core Concepts & Test Anatomy](../guides/getting-started/core-concepts-and-anatomy.md)**: Understand the fundamental building blocks of test suites and fixtures.

These resources will refine your skills, improve test reliability, and make your test code more maintainable.

## Advanced Topics and Optimization
